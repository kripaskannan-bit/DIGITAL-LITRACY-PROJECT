Prevention Checklist (Stay Safe Online)

General safety tips:
-Always verify the sender’s email address
-Do not click on suspicious links
-Use strong and unique passwords
-Enable two-factor authentication
-Never share OTPs or passwords
-Check website URLs before logging in
-Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions
-Keep your device and apps updated

UPI Safety:
-Double-check UPI ID before sending money
-Never approve unknown payment requests
-Do not authorize unknown or unexpected payment requests  
-Do not scan random QR codes from unknown people

Reporting Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
